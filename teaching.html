ABOUT RESEARCH TEACHING SOCIAL IMPACT CONTACT

To date I have delivered lectures and seminars for BA and MA students in UK and Sweden. Also, I have supervised BA and MA theses at Lund University and one at University of Ljubljana. In terms of substantive topics, I have taught courses in comparative political science, public policy, development theories and social science research methods. I have received pedagogical training on Learning and Teaching in Higher Education in the UK (2018) and Sweden (2021-2022).

As a teacher I often assume the role of a guide and fellow traveller who helps the students discover and make sense of new knowledge. Hence, in my teaching I put emphasis on experiential learning and active and inclusive engagement.
