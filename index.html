ABOUT RESEARCH TEACHING SOCIAL IMPACT CONTACT
Milka Ivanovska Hadjievska

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the research programme ‘Civil Society Elites? Comparing elite composition, reproduction, integration and contestation in European civil societies’ at Lund University, Sweden. Previously, I was part of the ERC-funded ‘Regulating Civil Society Project' at the University of Exeter, UK. 

The bulk of my research focuses on the legal regulation, governance and leadership of civil society organizations (CSOs) in Europe. My publications appear in leading peer-reviewed social science journals, including Political Studies, Journal of Civil Society and Voluntary Sector Review. I have presented my reserch at international social science conferences such as the ECPR General conference (Oslo, 2018; Innsbruck, 2022), the ECPR Joint Sessions of Workshops (Mons, 2019), the Political Studies Association Annual General Conference (Cardiff, 2018; Online, 2021 and 2022), CES (Lisbon, 2022) and others.

I hold a PhD in Politics from the University of Exeter (2019), MA in Political Science from the Central European University (2014) and MA in Human Resource Management in Culture and Non-governmental Organisations from University Ss. Cyril and Methodius (2012).
