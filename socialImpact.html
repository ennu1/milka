ABOUT RESEARCH TEACHING SOCIAL IMPACT CONTACT

I started my career in think tank organisations in the Western Balkans, where I have gained experience in policy research and advocacy activities. To date I have co-authored several policy briefs and reports. I have maintained contacts with key stakeholders working in the area of civil society development in North Macedonia and the Western Balkans. I have also organised and participated in several events for stakeholders, presenting academic and policy research for diverse audiences.
